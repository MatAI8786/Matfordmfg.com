Precision CNC & Turnkey Finishing

Full-Spectrum Machining

From quick-turn prototypes to small-batch production, Matford’s NLX-series turning centres take parts from raw bar to finished geometry in a single setup. Live-tool 4-axis milling, deep-hole drilling and tight in-house QC mean you get consistent accuracy — and a lead time counted in days, not weeks.

Finish, Pack & Deliver

Need more than metal-cutting? We coordinate heat-treat (harden, temper, nitride), coatings (zinc, Geomet®, Xylan®) and full press-work or welded sub-assemblies — then bundle matched nuts, bolts and gaskets so every kit arrives production-ready at your door, anywhere in the UK.
